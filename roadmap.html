1st Year Engineering Roadmap

Welcome to the thrilling world of engineering! Your first year is a crucial stepping stone.


  • Focus on fundamental courses in mathematics, physics, and introductory engineering subjects.
  • Participate in orientation programs to get acquainted with your campus and fellow students.
  • Explore different engineering disciplines to make an informed decision about your major.

2nd Year Engineering Roadmap

Congratulations on completing your first year! Now, it's time to delve deeper into core engineering subjects.


  • Take advanced courses in engineering mechanics, circuit theory, materials science, and thermodynamics.
  • Engage in hands-on projects and lab work to apply theoretical knowledge in practical scenarios.
  • Join engineering clubs and professional societies to connect with like-minded peers.

3rd Year Engineering Roadmap

Specialize in chosen field:


  • Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning
  • Web Development
  • Software Engineering

Engage in collaborative projects with fellow students.

Prepare for graduation and explore career opportunities.
